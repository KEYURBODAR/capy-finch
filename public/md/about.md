# About Finch

Finch is a clean, fast, privacy-respecting front-end for Twitter/X.

## Features

- Browse profiles, timelines, search, and media without logging in
- Follow accounts and build lists — stored locally on your browser
- Articles, Attention feed, RSS for every surface
- No JavaScript tracking, no ads, no sign-up required
- Export your data as JSON, Markdown, or plain text
